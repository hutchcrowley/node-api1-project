#Essentialism  
------
###Do less, accomplish more
Essentialism is an app that is intentded to help people manage their most precious resource, time. Based upon the principle of mindfully choosing one's activities so as to maximize productiveness and meaningfulness of tasks versus time and effort spent. It's a different kind of productivity app. One that places your values first and foremost, while tackling the problem of time management with a holistic approach.
